My Quectel EC25-EUX Adventure on OPNsense

Hey there! I just got my Quectel EC25-EUX LTE modem working with OPNsense, and I wanted to share how it went. If you’re thinking about doing the same, here’s what I did (and what I learned along the way)!

Getting Things Plugged In

  • Popped the EC25-EUX into the mini PCIe slot (it fits snug!).
  • Hooked up the antennas and slid in the SIM card.

Setting Up OPNsense

  1. Checked if the modem showed up using dmesg and usbconfig (always a good sign when it does!).
  2. Went to Interfaces > Point-to-Point and set up a PPP interface.
  3. Typed in my carrier’s APN info (double-check this!).
  4. Turned on the interface and crossed my fingers—success!

Handy Tips & Gotchas

  • If it’s not showing up, check your cables and BIOS settings (I had to wiggle mine a bit).
  • Try cu -l /dev/cuaU0 to send AT commands and see if the modem responds.
  • If PPP is grumpy, peek at /var/log/ppp.log for clues.

So far, the EC25-EUX has been awesome for LTE failover. If you get stuck or just want to chat about it, drop me a line!
